Christmas BattleBot

The 10kg block of steel on wheels


As a fun way to cap off the year, Chironix hosted an internal robot wars style tournament one year. The rules were non-existent, weight classes consisted of a single one-size-fits-all class, and prizes were purely bragging rights.
My entry was an excuse for me to learn how to stick weld angle iron into a box frame and attach some wheelbarrow wheels (though it had proper ackermann steering geometry with uprights connected via a tie rod). The robot was rear wheel drive via a single stage pulley gear reduction, actually using parts from the remains of my v1 electric longboard which had since worn out after the 1000+ kilometres I had put on it.
